Manali Trip

A 9 days trips - Best realization for you

ITINERARY

Day 1

DELHI - SHIMLA (365 KM)

Arrive Delhi airport train station and drive to Shimla. One of the most popular hill Resorts of the North.

Day 2

Shimla

After having breakfast proceed for sightseeing tour of Shimla. Visit Kufri, Ridge Maidan, Mall Road and Christ Church.

Day 3

Shimla – Manali (270 km)

After having breakfast proceed for Manali. Manali is an important hill station of northern India and is the destination

Day 4

Manali - Rohtang Pass - Manali

Today full day excursion visiting Kothi Gorge, Gulaba, Marhi, Rohtang Pass (Upto Snow Line in case Rohtang is closed

Day 5

Manali – Chamunda – Dharamshala (260 km)

After breakfast, proceed for Dharamshala. En-route visit Chamunda Devi Temple. Check in to the hotel. Evening at leisure.

Day 6

Dharamshala – Dalhousie. (140 kms)

After breakfast visit Bhudhist Monastery, Bhagsunag Temple. Enjoy the scenic beauty of Dharamshala. Later drive to Dalhousie.

Day 7

Dalhousie - Khajjiar - Dalhousie

Afte Hving breakfast, visit Khajjiar (Khajjiar is closed some time in Dec-Jan due to heavy snow) the mini Switzerland,

Day 8

Dalhousie- Chandigarh (300 kms)

After breakfast proceed to Chandigarh. On arrival check in to the hotel. Evening free to explore the local markets.

Day 9

Chandigarh to Delhi (250 kms)

After having breakfast, sightseeing of Chandigarh visit Rockgarden. Rose Garden and Sukhna Lake and transfer to Delhi.
